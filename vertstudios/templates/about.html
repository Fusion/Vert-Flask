About Us

Vert Studios is a web and graphic design company located in beautiful Tyler, Texas.

We take the time to learn the latest technologies as they appear - staying a step ahead of our competition, and allowing you to do the same.

Our Company

Born out of a love for quality web design and superior functionality, Vert Studios was founded by Justin Edwards in 2009. Identifying the need for a higher design standard in East Texas, Justin opened shop - bringing years of web development experience for a multi-billion dollar energy corporation with him. But he didn' t stop there. Good design becomes great when paired with top-notch web programming, and co-founder Joseph McCullough tailors his development skills based on the business goals you're looking to achieve.

Template websites are becoming a thing of the past, and Vert Studios is here to help you create a custom website you can call your own. Give us a call today, and let us know how we can help you make that all-important first impression.

Our Philosophy

Quality

We love cookies, but hate the cutter.

The word "template" doesn't exist in our vocabulary. We know what it means to stand out from the crowd. In today's world, differentiation is not only good business practice, it's downright necessary.

When you come to Vert Studios, you receive quality, custom design - a website you can call your own. So go ahead, let your competition have that cookie cutter template while you turn up the heat with new business. They'll just be left with crumbs.

Communication

We're here for you. Anytime.

You won't ever get that nerve-grating "robot-speak" when you give us a call. Instead, you'll be pleasantly surprised to find real people on the other end of that receiver - because while technology is a lovely thing, talking to it is not.

Relationships

Face-time used to actually mean something.

That's not an issue here, as you'll be doing business with either one of us - Justin or Joseph - two native East Texans who truly understand where you're coming from.
