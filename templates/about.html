Our Company

Born out of a love for quality web design and superior functionality, Vert Studios was founded by Justin Edwards in 2009. Identifying the need for a higher design standard in East Texas, Justin opened shop - bringing years of web development experience for a multi-billion dollar energy corporation with him. But he didn' t stop there. Good design becomes great when paired with top-notch web programming, and co-founder Joseph McCullough tailors his development skills based on the business goals you're looking to achieve.

Template websites are becoming a thing of the past, and Vert Studios is here to help you create a custom website you can call your own. Give us a call today, and let us know how we can help you make that all-important first impression.

Our Philosophy

Quality

The word "template" doesn't exist in our vocabulary. We know what it means to stand out from the crowd. In today's world, differentiation is not only good business practice, it's downright necessary.
